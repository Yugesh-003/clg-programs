Top 5 colleges/universities in India

  1. IIT - Madras

    IIT Madras is regarded as one of India's top engineering institutions. The Indian Institute of Technology Madras is recognized both nationally and globally for excellence and competence in technical education, fundamental and applied research, innovation, entrepreneurship, and consultancy in the industrial areas.

    Courses.

    • Architecture and planning.
    • Banking, Finance, and Insurance.
    • Business and Management studies.
    • Engineering.
    • Humanities and social science.


  2. VIT, Chennai

    It was created under Section three of the UGC- University Grants Commission Act, 1956, and it was launched in the year 1984 as a self-sustained college named the Vellore Engineering College. The Union Ministry of HR Development granted University status to Vellore Engineering College in the year 2001. The Institution is led by its founder and Chief Executive, Dr. G. Viswanathan, a veteran Parliament member & Minister in the Government of Tamil Nadu state. In acknowledgment of his contribution to India in providing quality education, he was granted an honorary degree by USA, West Virginia University. Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai was formed in 2010.

    Courses.

    • BBA
    • BCA
    • B.Tech.
    • B.Sc


  3. NIT, Tiruchirappalli

    NIT Tiruchirappalli, also known as NIT Trichy, is a public research and technical institution in Tamil Nadu, India, not far from the city of Tiruchirappalli. Both governments of India and Tamil Nadu established this institution in the year 1964 as the Regional Engineering College Tiruchirappalli, which was affiliated with the University of Madras.

    Courses.

    • Bachelor of Technology
    • Bachelor of Architecture
    • B. Sc. B. Ed.
    • Master of Technology


  4. CMC, Vellore

    Christian Medical College, Vellore, sometimes referred to as CMC, Vellore, is a privately owned medical college, hospital, and research facility run by the local Christian community. In and around Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India, this Institute has a network of basic, secondary, and tertiary care facilities.

    Courses.

    • MBBS
    • MD Pharmacology
    • MD Biochemistry
